Add these under Discord Developer Portal -> Your Application -> OAuth2 -> Redirects

LOCAL
http://localhost:3000/api/auth/callback/discord

VERCEL
https://YOUR-PROJECT.vercel.app/api/auth/callback/discord

CUSTOM DOMAIN (optional)
https://YOUR-DOMAIN.com/api/auth/callback/discord

Rules:
- Use the exact domain used by NEXTAUTH_URL.
- Do not add a trailing slash.
- The Client Secret belongs only in Vercel environment variables.
